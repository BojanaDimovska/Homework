Hi guys, my name is Bojana I'm coming from a small town in Macedonia called Veles and I'm 22.

I made this page in honor of art and these beautiful Japanese creations called Anime.

In general I love to draw and I also love and enjoy reading Mangas and watching Anime, that's basically how I spend all of my free time.

Japan is one of my favourite places on earth and I would love to live there someday.
